Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our car rental services
What documents do I need to rent a car?

Answer: Usually a valid driver's license and ID.

Is there a minimum age requirement for renting?

Answer: Yes, the minimum age is 25.

What happens if the car breaks down during my rental?

Answer: We provide 24/7 roadside assistance.

Can I rent a car for someone else?

Answer: No, the renter must be the driver.

Can I return the car in a different location?

Answer: Yes, but additional fees may apply.
